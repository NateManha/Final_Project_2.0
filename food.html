Food

Breakfast

Some common meals for breakfast in Granada are:

  • Toast with tomato, olive oil
  • Toast with butter or jam
  • Tortilla de patatas (Potato omelet)

Lunch

Below are some of the best lunches you can have in Granada:

  • La tortilla del Sacromonte (omelet with sweetbread and ham)
  • La pipirrana (Salad)
  • paella

Dinner

Dinners in Granada can include:

  • Habas con Jamón (Beans and Ham)
  • Jamón de Trevélez (Cured Ham)
  • olla de San Antón (Pork Strew)

Dessert

Some traditional desserts from Granada are:

  • Piononos de Santa Fe (Pastries)
  • Soplillos de la Alpujarra (Sweet Bread)
  • Huesos De Santo (Almond and cooked Egg yolks)
